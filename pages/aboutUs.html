Our Story

The Dragonfly Inn was born from a journey—one that began in the heart of Minas, Uruguay, in the peaceful Valley of the Thread of Life. During a quiet visit to this natural sanctuary, the founders felt something shift: an unmistakable sense of connection, purpose, and serenity that only certain places in the world can offer. Surrounded by rolling hills, native forests, and the calming sound of flowing water, they dreamed of creating a retreat that captured the same grounding energy of the valley. A place where guests could slow down, breathe, and rediscover the simple beauty of everyday moments. That dream took shape slowly, with patience and love. An old property—full of charm, stories, and a timeless soul—became the perfect space to bring the vision to life. Renovated with care, the building now blends rustic textures, soft natural tones, handcrafted details, and quiet corners meant for reflection, rest, and meaningful conversations. Today, the Dragonfly Inn is a sanctuary for travelers seeking warmth, authenticity, and connection. Every room, every path, and every shared moment echoes the essence of the Valley of the Thread of Life: a reminder that life is woven from small threads of peace, community, and genuine hospitality. Here, time slows down. Nature breathes with you. And every stay adds a new thread to the story we continue to weave together.
